# March newsletter: Real users on why they switched + upcoming learning events
When I talk to Tailscale users online, at conferences, or in the wild, I hear lots of different stories of how they got started with the service. In some cases, Tailscale enabled them to do something they’d never realized was possible. In others, they had some existing legacy VPN solution and were starting to really run into the rough edges of it.
Nobody talks to more users in those two camps than Chris, our director of new business sales. In a recent blog post, Chris distills some of the [business challenges and pain points](https://tailscale.com/blog/patterns-from-the-field-pain-points?utm_source=hs_email&amp;utm_medium=email&amp;_hsenc=p2ANqtz-96bwgPMLeHQqRGAye6O9My5u1JKJHdJGXQEgZikRL6INfjted_e_LVKKo-nYcn3guJ3vcd) that teams face before kicking off an evaluation of Tailscale. Sometimes it’s exciting news, like growing business operations (🎉). Sometimes it’s tending to team members getting grumpy with legacy VPN performance problems (😡).
If any of those sound familiar, you may be interested in an upcoming [free, no-pitch webinar about migrating to Tailscale](https://tailscale.com/events-webinars/replace-your-legacy-vpn?utm_source=hs_email&amp;utm_medium=email&amp;_hsenc=p2ANqtz-96bwgPMLeHQqRGAye6O9My5u1JKJHdJGXQEgZikRL6INfjted_e_LVKKo-nYcn3guJ3vcd). You’ll find more info about upcoming events, alongside our regular updates and community links, in this month’s edition of the newsletter.
