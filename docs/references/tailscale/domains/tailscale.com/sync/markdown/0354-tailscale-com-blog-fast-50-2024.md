# Tailscale named in Deloitte’s Technology Fast 50
Tailscale has been recognized in [Deloitte’s Technology Fast 50](https://www2.deloitte.com/ca/en/pages/fast-50/topics/canadas-technology-fast-50-program.html) as one of Canada’s fastest-growing tech innovators. It’s an honour to be included alongside other companies that are pushing boundaries, both abroad and right here at home.
### [Rooted in Canada, built for the world 🇨🇦](#rooted-in-canada-built-for-the-world)
Tailscale started in Canada with the belief that everyone, from individuals to enterprises, will benefit from accessible and secure networking that “just works”. From small beginnings, we’ve grown thanks to the dedication of our team and the trust of our customers, both here and around the world. While there is much more work ahead, we are proud to have been acknowledged for all of our efforts and impact so far.
Being in Deloitte’s spotlight means a lot, especially considering the other incredible companies included in the Fast 50. It’s a testament to the hard work our team puts in each day and the trust our users place in us. Plus, it’s not every day that you get to call yourself one of the fastest-growing tech companies in Canada!
This award from Deloitte reflects the progress we’ve made and the meaningful work our team continues to do. Our vision is to make secure networking effortless and accessible for everyone. With each milestone, we’re moving closer to bringing this vision to life.
### [Join us in building the future of networking](#join-us-in-building-the-future-of-networking)
As we continue growing in Canada and beyond, we’re looking for people who believe in the potential of a truly secure and flexible internet. If you’re interested in being part of the team that’s dedicated to transforming networking then [**take a look at our open roles**](https://tailscale.com/careers). There’s never been a more exciting time to build the future with us at Tailscale.
Thank you to Deloitte for this recognition, and a huge thank you to everyone in the Tailscale community who has supported us along the way. We’re excited for what’s to come.
