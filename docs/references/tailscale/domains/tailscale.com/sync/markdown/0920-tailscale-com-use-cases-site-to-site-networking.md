## Modernize site-to-site networking
### Go a step further from traditional site-to-site configurations
Tailscale gives you the option of a secure, high-performance mesh network. Use Tailscale's mesh network with its 4via6 feature for automatic handling of overlapping IP and CIDR ranges in subnets, ensuring unambiguous IPv6 addresses.

Just add a subnet router to each subnet and connect them to give access to apps, IDEs, and other nodes on your tailnet.
