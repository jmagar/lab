Even with all the funding news, the world hasn’t stopped — we have a handful of community contributions and Tailscale improvements to walk through.
Let’s jump in:
### What’s new and improved
* [**Sync Okta groups to use in your Tailscale ACLs**](/blog/sync-okta-groups/)
You can sync [group membership](https://help.okta.com/en/prod/Content/Topics/users-groups-profiles/usgp-about-groups.htm) and [deactivated users](https://help.okta.com/en/prod/Content/Topics/users-groups-profiles/usgp-deactivate-user-account.htm) from Okta, and refer to a synced group in your ACL file.
* [**User & Group Provisioning for Okta in Tailscale Demo**](https://www.infoworld.com/article/3648050/12-ways-to-slash-your-cloud-costs.html) [video, English]
Ramya Nagarajan offers a video tutorial for Okta group sync.
* [**Ask a Tailscale Engineer: What is User & Group Provisioning for Okta?**](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5bxU3Kltd1g&amp;t=1s) [video, English]
We sat down with Ramya Nagarajan to give more information about user and group provisioning for Okta.
* [**Tailscale authentication for NGINX**](/blog/tailscale-auth-nginx/)
For those who use NGNIX and Tailscale, we’ve documented how to glue the two together and use Tailscale to auth your services behind NGINX.
* [**Android TV remote control**](/blog/android-tv/)
In a guest post for a much-requested topic, Elias Naur details running Tailscale on Android TV.
* [**Tailscale authentication for Minecraft**](/blog/tailscale-auth-minecraft/)
Learn how to make your Minecraft server join your tailnet and authenticate to it with Tailscale.
