### Can I resume interrupted file transfers with Taildrop?
If your large file transfer is interrupted, you can have Taildrop try to resume the transmission where it left off. Most document transfers can be resumed for up to one hour after failure. Transfers can be resumed on all platforms unless a macOS or iOS device is receiving the file.
### How do I securely transfer sensitive documents from my computer to my mobile device?
Taildrop lets you transfer sensitive files (such as medical documents) between any of your connected devices via an encrypted peer-to-peer connection. This guarantees only the machines accessing the files during transfer are the sending and receiving devices.
