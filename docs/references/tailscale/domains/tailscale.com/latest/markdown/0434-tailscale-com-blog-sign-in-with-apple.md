# Sign in to Tailscale with Apple
Users can now sign in to Tailscale using their Apple ID. For users who favor having their credentials managed by Apple in iCloud, this is a great choice.
On the Mac and iPhone, signing in to Tailscale with your Apple ID takes advantage of TouchID and FaceID, so signing in is even faster. On other devices, signing in with your Apple ID is available via an OIDC web flow. This addition rounds out Tailscale’s support for major identity providers, alongside Google, GitHub, Microsoft, Okta, custom OIDC providers, [and more](/kb/1013/sso-providers/).
To create or sign in to a tailnet with your Apple ID, go to [tailscale.com/start](/start) and select “Sign up with Apple”.
