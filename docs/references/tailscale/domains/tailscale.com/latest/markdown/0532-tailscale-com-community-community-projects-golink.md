# golink
golink is a private link-shortening and redirect service that operates entirely within an organization's Tailscale network, improving internal resource discovery and navigation.
At Tailscale, we use golink internally for creating memorable and shareable shortcuts to internal resources on our corporate tailnet, as well as to URLs on the public internet. We have configured permissions for admin access through ACL grants.
We outlined some of our more interesting link templates in the blog post announcing golink's release.
