# Tailscale crossword: Any port in a storm
Did you know that December 21 is National Crossword Puzzle Day? It’s true; we looked it up. It’s the anniversary of when the first crossword puzzle was published in the New York World.
Many of us at Tailscale are puzzle aficionados. We’ve even got a #puzzles channel in our Slack to share our daily [Puzzmo](https://www.puzzmo.com) scores and solve times. So we decided to mark this holiday — how else? — with a brand new Tailscale crossword puzzle. We're publishing a day early to give you extra time to solve.
This one’s a bit tricky: if you’re a solver of the New York Times daily, you might find this to be comparable to a Wednesday or Thursday. We’ve got an embedded solver below, or you can [download a PDF](https://cdn.sanity.io/files/w77i7m8x/production/55dec3e89a2a87eaeb2b3ab3ccef184fe3d5f1d4.pdf) if you want to print it out and solve on paper.
How'd you do? Let us know on [Mastodon](https://hachyderm.io/@tailscale), [Bluesky](https://bsky.app/profile/tailscale.com), or [X](https://x.com/tailscale) (formerly Twitter).
