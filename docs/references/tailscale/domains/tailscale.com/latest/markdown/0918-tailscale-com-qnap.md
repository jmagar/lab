## File sharing is cumbersome. It is expensive. Even if you are an IT wizard (without the funny hat) it’s hard to secure. Tailscale for QNAP NAS changes that:
* Download Tailscale from the [QNAP App Center](https://www.qnap.com/en/app_center/con_show.php?op=showone&amp;internalName=Tailscale&amp;qts=5.1.0) for access from anywhere, without opening firewall ports.
* [Share](https://tailscale.com/kb/1084/sharing/) your QNAP NAS with designated Tailscale users.
* Restrict access [using ACLs](https://tailscale.com/kb/1018/acls/).
* Use your NAS as [a subnet router](https://tailscale.com/kb/1019/subnets/) to provide external access to your LAN, replacing a traditional standalone VPN server.
* Use your QNAP NAS as [an exit node](https://tailscale.com/kb/1103/exit-nodes/) for safe Internet access even when you’re in an untrusted location like an Internet cafe.
