## Student offer
Verified university students in the United States and Canada can
claim $100 in ChatGPT credits to use in Codex. One offer per
student, subject to successful verification.
By activating this offer, you agree to the [Service Credit Terms](https://openai.com/policies/service-credit-terms/) and the eligibility requirements below. Once verified, credits are automatically
added to your ChatGPT account for use in Codex.
[ Claim now ](https://chatgpt.com/codex/offers/students)
## How it works
1.
Start the verification flow through the claim page using your
ChatGPT account and university email.
2.
Once verification succeeds, OpenAI applies $100 in ChatGPT credits
to that account for use in Codex.
3.
Use the credits before they expire 12 months after the grant date.
## Eligibility Requirements
This offer is available to current students enrolled at
degree-granting universities in the United States or Canada and
residing in the United States or Canada when claiming the offer.
Eligibility is subject to successful verification. Limit one credit
offer per student.
## Frequently asked questions
Who is eligible for this offer?
This offer is available to current students enrolled at
degree-granting universities in the United States or Canada and
residing in the United States or Canada when claiming the offer.
Eligibility is subject to successful verification. Limit one
credit offer per student.
Click "Claim now" above to begin the verification
process.
Does my ChatGPT account need to be associated with my school?
No. You can use a personal ChatGPT account to claim the offer. You
still need to verify student status during the claim process.
How do I redeem the credits?
Once you complete verification, credits are automatically applied
to your ChatGPT account. The credits can be used in Codex and
expire 12 months after the grant date.
How do I see my credits?
You can view your credit balance on the [usage page](https://chatgpt.com/codex/settings/usage). Once the offer is applied, you should see 2,500 credits, which
is equivalent to $100.
Are these API credits?
No. These are ChatGPT credits applied to your ChatGPT account for
use in Codex. Learn more about ChatGPT credits in the [
Help Center
](https://help.openai.com/en/articles/12642688-using-credits-for-flexible-usage-in-chatgpt-freegopluspro-sora).
Do I need a ChatGPT account?
Yes. You need a ChatGPT Free, Go, Plus, or Pro plan to get
started. After verification, credits are applied to that account.
The credits can then extend Codex usage beyond the limits included
in your plan.
Do credits expire?
Yes. Credits expire 12 months after the grant date. Unused credits
after that date are no longer available.
Do I need to make a separate Codex account?
No. Codex is part of your ChatGPT account. If you do not already
have a ChatGPT account, create one before claiming the offer.
