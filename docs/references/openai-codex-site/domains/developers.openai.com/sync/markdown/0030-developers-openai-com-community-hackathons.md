We partner with organizers to support independent, community-led
hackathons, from developer meetups and student groups to
accelerators and startup ecosystems.
[ Apply for Support ](https://openai.com/form/hackathon-support)
## What we provide
Support is tailored to each event and may include:
* Codex access
* OpenAI API credits
* Prizes for top projects
* Workshop materials, developer resources, and starter kits
* In-person or remote mentoring and judging
* Amplification across OpenAI developer channels
## Who this is for
Organizers running builder-focused hackathons with clear
programming, active mentorship, and a plan to help participants ship
working demos:
* Developer communities and meetups
* VCs, accelerators, and startup ecosystems
* Universities and student groups
* Nonprofits and civic tech communities
## What we look for
We prioritize events with:
* Clear programming and judging structure
* Operational readiness from organizers
* Strong builder outcomes
## Important timeline
Apply at least one month before your hackathon date to allow time
for review and coordination.
Submitting an application does not guarantee support. Due to
application volume, we may not be able to respond to every request.
