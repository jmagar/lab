On this page
OpenACP connects to your team's messaging platform and routes messages to AI coding agents running on your machine. Choose the platform your team already uses to get started.
##
[](#choose-your-platform)
Choose Your Platform
Platform
Best for
Setup time
[Telegram](/platform-setup/telegram)
Personal use, solo developers, mobile-first workflows
\~5 minutes
[Discord](/platform-setup/discord)
Developer teams with existing Discord servers
\~10 minutes
[Slack](/platform-setup/slack)
Enterprise teams, organizations on Slack
\~15 minutes
Each platform requires you to create a bot or app in that platform's developer portal, then paste the credentials into `\<workspace\>/.openacp/config.json`. The interactive setup wizard (`openacp`) guides you through the process step by step.
After completing your platform setup, return to [Getting Started](/getting-started/getting-started) to connect an agent and run your first session.
