# Free and easy binary newsreader
### It runs anywhere
SABnzbd runs on [Windows](/downloads), [macOS](/downloads), [Unix](/wiki/installation/install-unix) and [NAS devices](/wiki/installation/install-nas).
You can access and manage SABnzbd from any device with a web browser, including iOS and Android phones and tablets.
### Small and large screens
With the responsive Glitter interface, you can access SABnzbd and manage your downloads from any device: desktop, tablet or phone. Others have made Android ([nzb360](https://nzb360.com/)) and iOS ([Sable](https://apps.apple.com/app/sable/id6630387095)) apps to manage SABnzbd.
### Integrates with other tools
Apps like [Sonarr](https://sonarr.tv/), [SickChill](https://sickchill.github.io/), [Radarr](https://radarr.video/), [Headphones](https://github.com/rembo10/headphones), [Lidarr](https://lidarr.audio/) and [more](/wiki/extensions-for-sabnzbd) can integrate with SABnzbd and automate your download process.
SABnzbd can also read and process [RSS feeds](/wiki/configuration/5.0/rss), activate [custom scripts](/wiki/configuration/5.0/scripts/post-processing-scripts) and [notify](/wiki/configuration/5.0/notifications) you.
### In 16 languages
SABnzbd now ships with **16 localizations**:
English, Dansk, Deutsch, Español (castellano), Français, Nederlands, Norsk bokmål, Polski, Português Brasileiro, Româna, Suomi, Svenska, русский язык, српски, עִבְרִית‎&lrm;, 简体中文.
