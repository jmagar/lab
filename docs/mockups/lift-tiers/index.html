Aurora / Lift Tier Study

Should elevation be uniform or tiered?

Both directions keep the same noticeable-lift panel language. The only change is whether all panels sit at one elevation level, or whether toolbars stay a little calmer while primary content panels lift harder.

Uniform Lift
